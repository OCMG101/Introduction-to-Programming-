CHICKEN CURRY RECIPIE

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken ,Skinned and portioned into chunks
  • 2 Red Onions
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 50g Garlic
  • 50g ginger
  • Chopped corriander
  • 5 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 2 tsp Corriander powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 2 tsp Tumeric
  • 100ml Coconut Milk
  • 100ml Yoghurt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 Lemon

    • METHOD

    STEP 1

    Put the chicken pieces in a bowl and add : Salt, Black Pepper, Cumin, Garam Masala, Yoghurt. Squeeze the lemon and drip the juice into the pot. Mix the ingredients in the pot to coat the chicken and leave it to marinate.

    STEP 2

    Thinly chop the onions and tomatoes. Grind the ginger and garlic in a mortar using a pestle until it becomes a paste.

    STEP 3

    Put oil in a pan and heat. Add onions and saute till golden brown. Add the ginger-garlic paste and saute till fragrant. Add : Black pepper, corriander, garam masala, tumeric. Cook to infuse the aromas into a paste. Add onions and saute breaking them down into a paste. Simmer till the oil starts to separate.

    STEP 4

    Add in the marinated chicken and cook in the curry base till coated. Simmer till there is oil separation and the chicken is infused with the masala.

    STEP 5

    Add in the coconut milk and water till preferred thickness. Mix and simmer to liking.

    STEP 6

    Add the chopped corriander, turn off the heat and mix to wilt the corriander. Serve with basmati rice.
