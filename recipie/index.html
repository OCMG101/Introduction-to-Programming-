FISH CURRY RECIPIE

Experience the tantalizing flavors of the sea with this mouthwatering Fish Curry recipe. This delectable dish combines the delicate and flaky goodness of fresh fish with a rich and aromatic curry sauce that will transport your taste buds to the coastal shores of exotic lands. With a perfect balance of spices and a hint of coconut milk, our Fish Curry is a culinary journey that promises to satisfy your craving for a hearty, savory, and spicy meal. Whether you're a seafood enthusiast or a curry lover, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. Let's dive into the art of creating this sumptuous dish that's perfect for any occasion!



INGREDIENTS

  • 500g pollock fillets, skinned
  • 2 White Onions
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 50g Garlic
  • 50g ginger
  • Chopped corriander
  • 5 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Corriander powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Tumeric
  • 100ml Coconut Milk
  • 100ml Yoghurt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 Lemon

METHOD

STEP 1

Thinly chop the onions and tomatoes.
Grind the ginger and garlic in a mortar using a pestle until it becomes a paste.

STEP 2

Put oil in a pan and heat.
Add onions and saute till golden brown.
Add the ginger-garlic paste and saute till fragrant.
Add : Corriander, garam masala, tumeric, cumin, salt.
Squeeze the lemon and drip the juice into the pan.
Cook to infuse the aromas into a paste.
Add onions and saute breaking them down into a paste.
Simmer till the oil starts to separate.

STEP 3

Add in the fish pieces and cook in the curry base till coated.
Simmer till there is oil separation and the chicken is infused with the masala.

STEP 4

Add in the coconut milk and water till preferred thickness.
Mix and simmer to liking.

STEP 5

Add the chopped corriander, turn off the heat and mix to wilt the corriander.
Serve with basmati rice.
